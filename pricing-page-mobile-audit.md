# Pricing Page Mobile Audit

Date: 2026-06-16

## Summary

The pricing page is directionally strong, but the mobile version still asks buyers to read too much before they understand the offer. The next variation should remove interaction choices, show all packages immediately, and make the funded-deal economics easier to compare.

## Recommended Changes

1. Remove the package selector.
   - Show Economy, Standard, and Premium by default.
   - On desktop, place the three package cards horizontally across the page.
   - On mobile, stack the cards vertically with the most important numbers near the top.

2. Remove the commission selector.
   - Use a fixed estimated commission of 10%.
   - Label it clearly as an assumption.

3. Shorten package card language.
   - Economy: Leads reporting $15K-$49,999/mo, lower-cost lead volume.
   - Standard: Leads reporting $50K-$99,999/mo, larger funding opportunities.
   - Premium: Leads reporting $100K+/mo, highest-revenue tier.

4. Make each package card lead with three numbers.
   - Price per lead.
   - Cost to fund one deal.
   - Estimated spread after lead cost.

5. Remove duplicate funded-rate language.
   - Use one clear label: Avg. LeadPop funded rate.

6. Simplify the education line.
   - Recommended copy: The goal is not to buy the cheapest lead. The goal is to buy leads where funded-deal revenue leaves enough spread after lead cost.

7. Simplify the sticky mobile CTA.
   - Use: Starts at $55/lead.
   - Button: Book Free Analysis.

8. Keep custom filters out of the main flow.
   - Mention at the bottom: We can price custom revenue, credit score, time in business, states, volume, and delivery setup on the call.

## New Variation Direction

Build a static pricing variation with no interactive package selector and no commission input. Use a fixed 10% commission assumption and display all three package cards side by side on desktop. On mobile, stack the cards and keep each card focused on the core economics.
