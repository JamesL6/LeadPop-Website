How We Calculate Your Offer

We believe in complete transparency. Here's exactly how we determine your cash offer:

  • After Repair Value (ARV) – What your home would sell for fully renovated
  • Minus Repair Costs – Estimated cost to bring it to market condition
  • Minus Our Margin – We're a business, but we're fair
  • = Your Cash Offer – Typically 70-85% of ARV
