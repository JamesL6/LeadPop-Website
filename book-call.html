Frequently Asked Questions

Is this a sales call?

+
No pressure tactics here. It's a strategy session to see if we're a good fit. If we can't help you, we'll tell you upfront.

How long is the call and how does it work?

+
Plan for 30 minutes. We'll meet via Zoom (preferred) so we can share screens and walk through your custom quote together. If you'd prefer a phone call instead, just let us know when booking.

What should I prepare?

+
Know your ideal lead criteria: credit score minimums, revenue thresholds, loan amounts you fund, and geographic targets. That helps us build an accurate quote.

What if I'm not ready to commit?

+
No problem. The call is zero commitment. Get your quote, think it over, and reach out when you're ready. No follow-up pressure.

Do you have minimum requirements?

+
We offer trial lead tests: a smaller batch of leads that fit your buy box so you can experience our quality firsthand. Once you see the results, you can scale up with confidence. No long-term commitment required to get started.

How soon can we start?

+
If we're a good fit, we can typically have your CRM integrated and campaigns live within a week of our call.
