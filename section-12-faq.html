Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know this isn't a scam?
Great question! We're a BBB A+ rated business with over 200 Google reviews. We close at a licensed local title company, and you can verify our business registration with the state. We're happy to provide references from past sellers.
Will I get a lowball offer?
We base our offers on actual market data and recent comparable sales. While our offers are typically 70-85% of ARV (to account for repairs we'll make), you're saving 6-10% in agent commissions, closing costs, and repair costs you'd pay in a traditional sale.
What's the catch? Why no fees?
No catch. We make our profit by renovating and reselling houses — not by charging you fees. When we buy your house below market value, we're taking on the risk and cost of repairs. It's a win-win!
How fast can you really close?
We can close in as fast as 7 days — but YOU choose the timeline. Some sellers need to move fast; others need 30-60 days. We work around your schedule.
Do I really not have to clean or remove my stuff?
Correct! Take what you want and leave the rest. We handle ALL cleanout and junk removal. We've bought homes filled floor-to-ceiling. Zero judgment, zero extra cost to you.
