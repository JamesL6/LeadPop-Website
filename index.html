FAQ

How do you ensure lead quality?

+
OTP verification confirms real contact info. Custom filters ensure leads match your buy box. AI optimization improves quality over time. Every lead is vetted before it reaches your CRM.

+
All major platforms ‚Äî Salesforce, HubSpot, Velocify, Zoho, and even Google Sheets. If you use it, we can push leads there in real-time.

+

+

+
Pricing varies based on loan type, qualification criteria, and volume. Book a call and we'll build a custom quote based on your specific needs and goals.

How does SMS OTP verification work?

+
When a prospect fills out our form, we send a one-time passcode via SMS to their phone number. They must enter this code to complete the submission. This confirms the phone number is real, active, and belongs to the person filling out the form ‚Äî eliminating fake numbers and bot submissions.

+
If a lead clearly doesn't meet your agreed-upon criteria ‚Äî wrong contact info, doesn't match your credit score minimum, revenue threshold, or other filters ‚Äî just flag it and we'll replace it at no charge. We review flagged leads quickly and don't make you jump through hoops.

+
Yes. If you specialize in certain verticals ‚Äî restaurants, trucking, medical practices, construction, etc. ‚Äî we can target those industries specifically. We can also exclude industries you don't fund.

What data fields do you capture on each lead?

+
Standard fields include: business name, owner name, phone, email, estimated credit score range, annual revenue, years in business, loan amount requested, and use of funds. We can customize the form to capture additional fields you need for your underwriting process.

How do you generate the leads?

+
We run targeted paid advertising campaigns across multiple platforms to reach business owners actively seeking financing. Our campaigns are optimized specifically for business loan intent, not recycled from other industries. We handle all creative, targeting, and optimization in-house.
